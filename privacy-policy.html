Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 9, 2025

Introduction

Stoic Lessons (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles user data and what permissions it requests.

We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal information from users.

Information Collection and Use

Our app is designed to work entirely offline and does not collect or transmit any data to us or any third party. We do not require user accounts, analytics, advertising tracking, or cloud data storage.

Permissions Used

To function properly, our app requests the following permissions:

  • Storage Access – Used to read and write app-related files or save necessary app content locally on your device. We do not access or upload personal files.
  • Notification Access – Used to display in-app reminders or alerts. No data is collected or transmitted.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Since no personal data is collected, there is no data to share, sell, or disclose.

Children’s Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect or request any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Security

Although we do not collect data, we follow best practices to ensure the app is secure and does not access more information than necessary for its core functionality.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect app improvements or regulatory updates. The latest version will always be available within the app and on our official page.
